# menus.jp

## Overview

**menus.jp** is a mobile-first SaaS platform that allows restaurant and salon owners in Japan to create a public business profile page in minutes — accessible via a single shareable link. The tagline is: *"Your business. One link. Done."*

---

## Supported Business Types

- Restaurant
- Hair Salon
- Barbershop
- Spa
- Gym

---

## Authentication

Users sign in with **Google Login** (OAuth 2.0). No separate email/password registration is required. The Google account is used to identify the owner and associate their business profile.

---

## Onboarding Flow — 4 Steps

### Step 1 / 4 — Create Your Business Profile

- User manually enters their business name
- Select business category: Restaurant / Salon / Barbershop / Spa / Gym
- No external data import — all information is entered by the user

### Step 2 / 4 — Enter Business Info

All fields are filled in manually by the user:

- **Business Name** — required
- **Category** — required: Restaurant / Salon / Barbershop / Spa / Gym
- **Address** — required
- **Phone Number** — required
- **Business Hours** — per day with open time, close time, and last order time; toggle to mark days as closed
- **Closed Days** — select any day of the week; custom days can be added
- **Website** — optional
- **Photos** — 1 hero/banner image + up to 6 additional gallery photos

### Step 3 / 4 — Set Up Booking & Social Links

**Booking Links:**
- Tabelog
- HOT PEPPER Gourmet
- LINE Reservation
- OpenTable
- Add other custom booking service

**Social Media Links:**
- Instagram
- Facebook
- X (Twitter)
- LINE Official Account
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Other (custom URL)

> Up to 4 booking links are displayed on the public page. Additional links appear under a "+ More" button.

### Step 4 / 4 — Go Live

- A unique public link is generated
- Share the link to publish the page instantly

## Public Page Features

### Common to All Business Types

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Language toggle | Switch between Japanese and English |
| Live open/closed status | Real-time indicator showing current status, hours, and last order time |
| Closed day display | Shows the weekly closed day on the page |
| Directions button | Links to map/navigation app |
| Call button | One-tap phone call |
| Booking buttons | Up to 4 displayed; extras grouped under "+ More" |
| Social follow section | Icons and links for all enabled social accounts |
| Footer bar | Address, phone number, and Open Map shortcut |
| Powered by badge | "Powered by menus.jp" shown on Free plan; hidden on Premium |

### Restaurant Page

- Menu category tabs: Lunch, Dinner, Drinks, Dessert, A La Carte (scrollable)
- Each menu item includes: photo, name in Japanese and English, price, short description, and a "Popular" badge for featured items
- Dark, premium visual theme

### Salon / Beauty Page

- Service tabs: Menu, Pricing, Staff, Gallery (scrollable)
- Each service item includes: photo, name in Japanese and English, duration, price, and a "Book" button
- Light, elegant visual theme with soft pink accents
- Additional booking platforms supported: HOT PEPPER Beauty, minimo

## Technical Notes

- Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 (Google Login)
- Business data: manually entered by the owner — no third-party data import
- WebP image format used for performance optimization
- Page size target: under 500KB
- Free plan: subdomain hosted on menus.jp (e.g. ushiya.menus.jp)
- Premium plan: supports custom domain (e.g. ushiya-shibuya.com)
- Responsive, mobile-first layout throughout
